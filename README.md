# BAJAJ-AUTO.pbix
This Bajaj-auto business Power BI project depicts line graph showing sum of turnover in lacks & sum of volume by year; pie graph showing sum of turnover in lacks by year; column graph showing sum of number of trades by date along with two cards showing sum of high & sum og low & a slicer for date.
Data source: Kaggle.
